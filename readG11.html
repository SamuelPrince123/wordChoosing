12 Words and Their Meanings

  • Airplane
    A flying vehicle with wings.
  • Bee
    A small insect that makes honey.
  • Bell
    A round object that makes sound.
  • Boat
    A vehicle that moves on water.
  • Bike
    A two-wheeled vehicle you pedal.
  • Bread
    Food made from flour and water.
  • Brush
    A tool for cleaning or painting.
  • Basket
    A container made of woven material.
  • Bear
    A large wild animal with fur.
  • Balloon
    A colorful inflatable object.
  • Biscuit
    A small baked sweet or savory snack.
  • Bun
    A small round bread roll.
